# CALIBERDESK MVP v1.0 - USER MANUAL & SYSTEM FLOW

## 1. INTRODUCTION
CaliberDesk is a next-generation social hiring platform designed to bridge the gap between talent and organizations using advanced AI, video interaction, and data-driven insights.

## 2. USER ROLES
- **Job Seeker:** Individuals looking for career opportunities and professional growth.
- **Employer (Organization):** Companies and recruiters looking to hire top talent efficiently.
- **Staff (Admin/Operator):** Internal operators managing platform integrity, verifications, and support. Roles include Super Admin, Sales Executive, Recruiter Head, and Finance Head.

## 3. USER FLOWS

### 3.1. Job Seeker Journey
1. **Onboarding:** 
   - Sign up via Email (requires verification) or Social OAuth (Google/LinkedIn).
   - Complete the Onboarding Wizard to set initial career goals and preferences.
2. **Profile Optimization:**
   - Build a "Neural Identity" including skills, digital certifications, and work history.
   - Record a **Video Introduction** to provide a human touch to the digital profile.
   - Use **CV Prep** and **Interview Prep** AI modules to refine application materials.
3. **Job Discovery:**
   - Browse the **Seeker Feed** for personalized job matches.
   - Use **Job Search Grounding** for real-time market data and deep searches.
   - View **AI Match Scores** to understand compatibility with specific roles.
4. **Application & Tracking:**
   - Apply to jobs with one-click "Initialize Session".
   - Track application status in **Seeker Applications**.
   - Engage with **Aptitude Tests** assigned by employers.
5. **Growth:**
   - Consult the **Career Coach** for AI-driven career pathing.
   - Monitor performance in **Seeker Analytics**.

### 3.2. Employer Journey
1. **Organization Setup:**
   - Register the company and add initial sub-users (Admin, Recruiter, Viewer).
   - **Verification Protocol:** Employers must verify via a corporate email domain or by uploading official business documents.
   - **Grace Period:** Unverified accounts are deactivated after 3 days of inactivity/non-verification.
2. **Talent Acquisition:**
   - Post job listings with detailed requirements and benefits.
   - Set up **Aptitude Tests** to filter candidates automatically.
3. **Candidate Management:**
   - Use the **Employer AI Copilot** to analyze candidate pools.
   - Review **Video Intros** and **AI Match Scores** for instant shortlisting.
   - Manage the team in **Organization Management**.
4. **Communication:**
   - Use **Employer Comm Assistant** to draft personalized outreach.
   - Engage in **Live Chat** with prospective hires.
5. **Insights:**
   - Track recruitment ROI and pipeline health in **Employer Analytics**.

### 3.3. Staff Journey
1. **System Oversight:**
   - Access the **Admin Dashboard** for a bird's-eye view of platform activity, including the **System Health Matrix** and **Global Activity Feed**.
2. **Verification Handling:**
   - Review and approve/reject company verification requests in the **Verification Desk**.
3. **User Management:**
   - Manage the global user directory via **User Identity Management**.
   - Provision internal staff access with specific operational roles (Sales, Recruiter, Finance).
4. **Support & Operations:**
   - Manage support requests via the **Service Desk Tickets** system.
   - Audit job listings in the **Job Oversight Desk**.
5. **Content Management:**
   - Publish industry insights and reports via the **Editorial Blog**.

## 4. NAVIGATION
The platform uses a modular layout with a sidebar for quick access to:
- **Feed/Dashboard:** Primary workspace.
- **AI Tools:** Suite of professional assistants.
- **Management:** Jobs, Applications, or Team settings.
- **Staff:** (Admin Only) Verification, Oversight, and User Management.
- **Insights:** Analytics and Blog.
- **Settings:** Profile and Billing management.
