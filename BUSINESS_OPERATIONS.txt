# CALIBERDESK MVP v1.0 - BUSINESS OPERATIONS & STRATEGY

## 1. REVENUE MODEL (MONETIZATION)
- **Seeker Premium:** Monthly subscription for advanced AI tools (Interview Prep, CV Optimization) and "Featured Profile" status.
- **Employer Tiers:**
  - **Basic:** Free job posting (limited).
  - **Pro:** Unlimited postings, AI Copilot access, and Aptitude Testing.
  - **Enterprise:** Custom integrations, dedicated account manager, and advanced analytics.
- **Pay-Per-Post:** One-time fee for single job listings for non-subscription users.
- **Featured Listings:** Premium placement for job posts to increase visibility.

## 2. OPERATIONAL WORKFLOWS
- **Customer Support:**
  - Tier 1: Automated AI Assistant for common queries.
  - Tier 2: Staff Portal for manual ticket handling and account issues.
- **Trust & Safety:**
  - Mandatory verification for all Employers before job posts go live.
  - Automated flagging for suspicious job descriptions or seeker behavior.
- **Content Strategy:**
  - Weekly "Insights" published to the Editorial Blog to drive SEO and authority.
  - Monthly "Market Reports" for Enterprise clients.

## 3. DATA PRIVACY & COMPLIANCE
- **GDPR/CCPA Ready:** All user data is stored with encryption at rest.
- **Role-Based Access:** Strict separation of data between different organizations and user types.
- **Consent Management:** Clear opt-in/out for marketing and data sharing.

## 4. SCALABILITY PLAN
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Built on a modular architecture ready for horizontal scaling.
- **Multi-Country Support:** The platform is designed to handle multiple currencies and localized job markets.
- **Async Processing:** Heavy tasks (AI analysis, video processing) are handled asynchronously to ensure UI responsiveness.

## 5. KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)
- **User Growth:** Monthly Active Users (MAU) for both Seekers and Employers.
- **Engagement:** Number of applications per job post and AI tool usage frequency.
- **Liquidity:** The ratio of active job posts to active seekers.
- **Retention:** Churn rate for premium subscriptions.
